These photos were taken in April 2015 at Mt. Wilson, a small village located in the Blue Mountains area of Sydney, Australia. Mt Wilson is only accessible by car, and is a 2 hour journey from the Sydney CBD.

Camera: Nikon D3200. Lenses: 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6G and 50mm f/1.8G.
